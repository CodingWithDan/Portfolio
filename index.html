About Me

I am a Los Angeles based software engineer who specializes in creating websites and full stack apps for both personal and professional with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, SQL, Node, MongoDB, and Express.

In addition to web design, I have a background in writing. So together we can shape copy and brand design for your online needs. I especially love working with small businesses to create a web presence that expands their reach.

Check out my projects below. If we sound like a good fit, let's get in touch!

My Process

Consultation
Do you need a website built, updated, or edited? Whatever your needs are, we'll start with a conversation and pinpoint what your specific needs and vision entail.

Proposal
Now that we know your needs, let's hash out the details and customize a proposal including a visual preview and a plan of action. We can figure out which proposal plan fits your needs and budget best.

Creation
Sit back and relax and let me do the leg work. You will receive regular updates regarding your project status while I create and test it.

Launch
Peace of mind is delivered with the final project: a user-friendly, responsive web page that satisfies your needs and perfectly fits your vision. Cheers!
